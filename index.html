Who We Are

TMC is a collection of like-minded Grass-root entrepreneurs focused on business growth and personal development. TMC’s objective is to enable middle-age grass-root business owners to be inspired and transformed from ailing business men and women to be captains of their respective industries

Our strength

We achieve this aim through our invaluable resources in form of conferences, workshop-leadership development programs (mentorship) and professional education opportunities, among other offerings specially designed for personal and professional growth.
