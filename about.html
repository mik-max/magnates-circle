History

History Of The Magnates Circle Initiatives

In the year 2018, a group of progressive minded young entrepreneurs formed a club of likeminded peer to help each other grow irrespective of their diversity in professionalism. We aimed at going against all odds, breaking all barriers to become captains in our individual chosen field or industries. Since the formation of the club, we have created our own path for continued personal and business growth.


At the end of the year 2019 our vision become more broad and clearer which necessitate the incorporation of the club. In March 2020, our club was officially incorporated as The Magnate Circle Initiative with an unwavering focus to be the Nigeria most influential community of entrepreneurs by the year 2025.
